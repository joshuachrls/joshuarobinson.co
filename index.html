I’m an interaction and user experience designer working in Chicago

I practice human-centered design thinking that initiates the communal development of ideas about the shape of the world.

I’m a champion for the user. I believe in helping people do what they already want to do, even when it requires a little "nudge." I want to make people happier, make households stronger, and make communities more vibrant through behaviorally effective design.

I’m also a writer, musician, husband and father: roles which have caused deeper reflection regarding extension and rebuilding and aided in my ability to adapt to new methods.
